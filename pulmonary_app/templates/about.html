About Respida AI

An intelligent, robust, and accessible pulmonary disease prediction platform.

Our Purpose

Respida AI is designed to assist individuals in identifying potential respiratory illnesses early through a combination of reported symptoms and advanced diagnostic machine learning, including cough audio analysis engines. The overarching goal is to bridge the gap between initial symptomatic onset and seeking professional clinical evaluation, ensuring users can proactively monitor their lung health.

Technologies Used

Python & Flask

Robust backend server architecture and API routing.

Machine Learning

SciKit-Learn & Librosa audio pattern logic networks.

JavaScript & SQLite/MongoDB

Dynamic DOM manipulation and persistent data storage.

Medical Disclaimer

This platform and its AI predictions are NOT a substitute for professional medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice. The results provided are for educational and informational purposes only based on aggregated health statistics. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, severe chest pain, or extreme difficulty breathing, please dial emergency services immediately and consult a certified healthcare professional.
